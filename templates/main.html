How It Works

1. Generate Keys

Generate your public and private key pair. Share your public key with others who want to send you encrypted messages.

2. Encrypt Messages

Use someone's public key to encrypt messages that only they can read with their private key.

3. Decrypt Messages

Use your private key to decrypt messages that others have encrypted with your public key.
